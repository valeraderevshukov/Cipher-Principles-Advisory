Trust

Trusted advice

The rise to prominence of cryptocurrencies has been accompanied by a corresponding level of fear, uncertainty, and doubt in the market, but we believe there is a sensible and appropriate response to this asset class that we will lead on.

As one of the first advisory services to come with licensing permission from the UK’s Financial Conduct Authority (FCA), our customers can take comfort in the fact we meet and exceed the highest standards in financial services anywhere in the world.

Experience

We know crypto

Cryptocurrencies aren’t just a new financial product, they are an entirely new technology that will change the way many businesses operate.

Buzzwords like blockchain and bitcoin are all over the news, but not many financial advisors have the knowledge and experience to capitalise on these truly unique developments. Cipher Principles is built on the collective understanding of some of the biggest minds in crypto. We leave no stone unturned when evaluating a new cryptocurrency - we analyse the underlying technology, we speak with the team behind it, evaluate their roadmap and conduct community surveys to gather consensus, from there we determine whether the coin has a profitable and sustainable future.

Approach

Scientific approach

More than half of our team come from academic backgrounds in mathematics, cryptography, and technology. We have an unprecedented and unrivaled understanding of what creates and drives value in cryptocurrencies.

Traditional portfolio construction approaches are inapplicable here. We employ advanced quantitative strategies, continually analysing the market to deliver the strategies and predictions you can build your portfolio on to maximise returns.

Advisory Service

Expertise

Our advisory services are designed primarily for cryptocurrency funds, as a custody and execution venue. But in addition, we service high networth individuals, family offices, and institutional investors who have the resources, but not necessarily the time or background to invest successfully in cryptocurrencies.

Understanding

We will take time to understand your priorities, goals, and targets before recommending an optimum portfolio structure. We understand that changes in the market or your own financial situation will affect your appetite for risk, and will constantly re-evaluate your portfolio, offering customised solutions that suit your changing needs.
